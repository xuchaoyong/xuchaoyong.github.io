Reading Note 2 of THE POWER of HABIT

Craving

How does the brain control yourself by habit?
cue, routine and reward will create a feeling of craving.
For example, when a smartphone vibrates with a new message, which has become a habit,
the brain starts anticipating the momentary distraction that opening an email provides. If you
do not check your phone deliberately, it might cause angry in your mind, because your craving
is not satisfied.

However, if someone disables the buzzing - and, thus, removes the cue - people can work for hours without
thinking to check their in-boxes.

  • When you have a habit, it has cause the corresponding craving in your mind.
